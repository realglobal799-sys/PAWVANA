Our Story

It started with a dog who refused to sleep anywhere but the sofa.

Our founder, Clara, had spent eleven years working in luxury interiors — obsessing over the details that make a space feel like a home. Then she got Biscuit, a rescue greyhound with anxious eyes and a tendency to knock things over.

Biscuit didn't care about thread counts or design awards. He cared about feeling safe. He cared about warmth. He cared about being close to the people he loved. And Clara realised that nothing she'd ever designed had mattered more than making that dog comfortable.

She started Pawvana in 2021 with a single question: what would a brand look like if it truly understood that a pet isn't a possession — they're a member of the family? Three years later, Pawvana is home to a community of over 48,000 pet parents who believe the same thing.

"We didn't set out to build a pet brand. We set out to honour a relationship."

Our Mission

To raise the standard of what pets deserve.

Pets can't tell us when something isn't right. They can't ask for better. They simply trust us — completely and without condition. That trust is the most humbling gift. Our mission is to make sure we're worthy of it.

🌿
Honest ingredients. No compromises.

Every material, formula, and component we use is chosen because it's genuinely good — not because it costs less. We read the research, consult the vets, and make the harder choice when it matters.

🏠
Spaces that make them feel at home.

A pet's environment shapes their wellbeing more than we often realise. We design with their instincts, comfort, and sense of security in mind — because a settled pet is a happy pet.

🤝
A community built on shared love.

We're building a movement of pet parents who hold themselves to a higher standard — who believe their animals deserve the same care they'd give anyone they love.

What We Believe

Five truths we refuse to compromise on.

These aren't values we wrote for a website. They're the principles that guide every decision we make — from a product formulation to a packaging choice to how we respond to a late-night message from a worried pet parent.

A
Pets are family. Full stop.

Not accessories. Not lifestyle props. Not "just animals." The bond between a pet and a person is one of the most consistent sources of unconditional love a human life can know. We treat that seriously — and we expect our products to reflect it.

B
Quality is an act of respect.

Choosing inferior materials because they're cheaper is a choice we'd never make for a person we love. Our pets deserve the same reasoning. When we cut a corner, we're cutting it on them — and that's not something we're willing to do.

C
Wellbeing isn't an add-on.

Physical health, emotional calm, mental stimulation — these aren't premium extras. They're the baseline. Every product we make is considered through the lens of what it does for a pet's whole self, not just how it looks on a shelf.

D
Transparency builds trust.

We tell you exactly what's in our products and why. We admit when something can be improved. We share the science, acknowledge the uncertainties, and never dress up a half-truth in confident language.

E
The planet is their home too.

Our pets live on this earth. Everything we do — from how we source our materials to how we ship our products — is considered through the lens of leaving something worth inheriting.

Our Promise

To every pet. To every person who loves them.

We know you've thought about this. You've read the label. You've wondered whether it's really worth it. You've asked if it's safe, if it'll last, if they'll actually like it.

We know because we've asked exactly the same questions. And our promise to you — and to them — is that we will never stop asking. We will never settle for good enough when better is possible.

Because they can't advocate for themselves. So we will.
